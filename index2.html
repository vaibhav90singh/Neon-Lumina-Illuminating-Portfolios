About Me

My story in a nutshell ;)

I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Application from Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), Delhi. Driven by a deep curiosity for how systems operate at scale, I've evolved my focus toward the modern tech landscape, specifically bridging the gap between Artificial Intelligence and robust Cloud Infrastructure.

During my academic journey, I gained valuable experience through diverse internships. This included a winter internship focused on computational neurology and a summer internship at IBM, where I worked extensively on AI and cloud technologies. I also interned at several prestigious companies such as CodeClause, Let's Grow More, and CodeSoft.

Today, I am passionate about Agentic AI, modern Full-Stack architectures (Next.js/Node), and DevSecOps. Whether it's setting up a Kubernetes cluster, fine-tuning an LLM, or securing APIs with Zero Trust architecture, I thrive on building solutions that are not just functional, but future-proof.
